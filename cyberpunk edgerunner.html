ANIMALS

A well-known gang from west Pacifica - The Animals - invented, then polished to perfection, a method of street fighting that lets them take on implanted opponents while using custom-brewed artisanal implants that eschew electronics. Their ferocity and love of battle make them prized bouncers and guards.
