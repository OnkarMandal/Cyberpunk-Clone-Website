Update 2.0 is now available for free to all players who own the base game

MAJOR CHANGES


  • POLICE SYSTEM
  • VEHILCLE COMBACT
  • CYBERWARE
  • PERKS
You might be Night City’s most infamous merc, but that doesn’t mean that you can sidestep the law. With the revamped police system, the NCDP will use new tactics to put a stop to your criminal activity. The more heat you get, the more aggressive officers will become by relentlessly chasing you down, deploying barricades, and unleashing stronger units onto the streets. Cause enough trouble, and you’ll be labeled as cyberpsycho, meaning the NCPD will send out its biggest guns to hunt you down: the fearsome MaxTac!
FAQ


  • What platforms will the update be available for?
    • The update is available for current-gen consoles — Xbox Series X|S and PlayStation 5 — and PC.

  • Do I need to own the Phantom Liberty expansion to get the update?
    • No, Update 2.0 is free to all players who own the base game of Cyberpunk 2077. Owning Phantom Liberty is not required.

  • Will I lose my progress in the game?
    • No, you will be able to keep playing from wherever you left off in the story.

  • How can I get Update 2.0?
    • If you already own Cyberpunk 2077 on Xbox Series X|S, PlayStation 5, or PC, Update 2.0 will automatically download on September 21st — provided you’re hooked up to the Internet! If you don’t own the game, you’ll need to purchase Cyberpunk 2077 in order to access Update 2.0.
